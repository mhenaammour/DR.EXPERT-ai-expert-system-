About Dr. Expert

Dr. Expert is an expert system designed to assist medical professionals with the diagnosis of various medical conditions. It uses a knowledge base of symptoms and their corresponding diagnoses to provide accurate and reliable suggestions for medical diagnosis.

Our system is designed to be user-friendly and efficient, allowing medical professionals to quickly and easily input patient symptoms and receive a list of potential diagnoses.

Dr. Expert is constantly updated with the latest medical research and findings to ensure that it provides the most accurate and up-to-date diagnoses possible.
